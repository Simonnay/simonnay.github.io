I’m a graphic designer based in Paris and I’m currently getting my art bachelor degree at Auguste Renoir. I was born in Montreal (Canada) and spent my life in between the Caribbean, France and Canada. Having lived in multiple continents made me fluent in both french and english. Moving away to study in France has greatly contributed to my independance.

Before studying graphic design I succesfully finished my first year of a philosophy bachelor degree. This year of learning broadened my perspective and gave me unique conception tools. Studying graphic design has given me a large cultural knowledge and all the technical tools needed to bring my ideas to fruition. I expanded my creative liberty by serving my community throught graphic design. I now try to reinvest everything I’ve learned and I see each project as an opportinity to do so. I beleive I can be an asset amongst a team of pluridisiplinary creatives and it would greatly contribute to my growth as a designer.
